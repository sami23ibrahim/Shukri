Section 5

The difference people describe

Conventional Therapy
Understanding
what is happening.

You recognise your pattern, can name it, trace it back to childhood. The mind has understood.

And yet it often remains a knowledge that does not carry you.

vs
Ketamine-Assisted Therapy
Experiencing
what is happening.

In a session, you feel your pattern. Not as a thought, but as an inner image, as a bodily sensation, as truth.

Insight does not come through analysis, it happens. And afterwards, change can often become easier, almost as if by itself.

Knowing, Feeling, Changing, in the same moment
