The Process

Four clear steps, carefully aligned, for a safe and supported experience.

Step 01
Consultation

An open initial conversation. Indication, contraindications, goals. No pressure, no predetermined outcome.

Step 02
Diagnostics

Vivecura holistic assessment. Heart, circulation, medication, psyche. Before anything is opened, risks are ruled out.

Step 03
Infusion

Sub-anaesthetic dose intravenously, around 80 minutes, with continuous 1:1 medical supervision. A calm setting, music, eye mask.

approx. 80 minutes
Step 04
Integration

Directly afterwards. Making sense of the experience, finding meaning, translating the next step into everyday life.

approx. 60 minutes
