Areas of Application

Typical Indications for Ketamine in Off-Label Use

In research and clinical practice, the following areas of application have become established for ketamine-assisted therapy.

Depression
Particularly when two or more antidepressants have not produced a response
Bipolar Depression
With close psychiatric supervision
Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder
PTSD, with accompanying psychotherapy
Anxiety Disorders
When conventional approaches reach their limits
Chronic Pain
Such as complex regional pain syndrome
Chronic Alcohol Use
When other approaches have been exhausted
Note

The use of ketamine in these areas is off-label, meaning outside its original approved indication. This approach is established in specialised centres and always requires individual medical assessment.
